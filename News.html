Saudi innovators harvest gold at the IENA International Fair in Germany

A number of technical college trainees were awarded gold, silver and bronze medals at the IENA International Innovation Fair, Dr. Ahmed Al-Fuhaid, Governor of the General Organization for Technical and Vocational Training, congratulated the trainees on the winners, pointing out the importance of this national achievement in the field of innovations and inventions by the youth of the homeland, who rely on them to achieve the aspirations of leadership under the unlimited support of wise leadership. The Foundation works hard to support and empower young people in its training facilities.Trainee Abdulaziz Khoja from the College of Communications and Electronics in Jeddah won the gold medal for inventing the smart waste container. Trainee Majed Al Harbi from Riyadh College of Technology received the silver medal for his innovation of automatic opening and closing. Its production of electricity using sand, while the trainee received Badr Al-Marri from the Technical College in Al-Kharj the bronze medal for his creation flex wheel adapter.Medals obtained by the delegation: 6 medals are: Two gold medals Two silver medals Two bronze medals
